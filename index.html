    Experience His Presence

    The bible says "They that walk with the wise shall be wise and the companion of fools shall be destroyed" (Prov 12:20). Your coming here today is an indication of wisdom and that you want to be a winner. When Saul found himself in the midst of Prophets, he began to prophesy (1 Sam 10:10). However, it takes an encounter with the word of God to win. Life without the word of God, the struggle continues. Coming once may not deliver to you the revelation and insight you need from the word of God. Constant and regular attendance will give you enough light that will transform your life from poverty, sickness, failure and frustration into the abundant life that Jesus promised you (John 10:10). As you keep coming, the Lord will establish you in all that you do. The Lord bless you real good! we love you. Peace!

    Come and see God do a new thing in your life this Sunday.
